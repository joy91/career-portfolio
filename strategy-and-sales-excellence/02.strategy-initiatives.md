# Strategy Initiatives

## Strategic Mandate
The Strategy team functioned as an **internal advisory unit**, supporting
senior leadership with data‑driven insights for long‑term business decisions.

---

## Key Activities

### Competitor Analysis
- Analyzed competitor offerings, pricing, and positioning
- Identified strategic threats and opportunities
- Provided structured insights for leadership reviews

### Startup & Investment Analysis
- Assessed startups for potential partnerships or investments
- Evaluated strategic fit, market potential, and risk
- Summarized findings for leadership decision‑making

### Market & Secondary Research
- Conducted secondary market analysis across geographies
- Identified growth opportunities and expansion feasibility

### Strategic Recommendations
- Contributed to C‑suite recommendations such as:
  - Creation of near‑shore offices
  - Market entry and expansion considerations

The focus was on **evidence‑based strategy**, not intuition.
