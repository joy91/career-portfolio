# Sales Excellence Enablement

## Objective
Enable sales teams with the **right tools, targets, insights, and governance**
to improve performance and predictability.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### CRM Enablement
- Maintained and updated the CRM platform
- Ensured data accuracy and usability
- Supported enhancements aligned with sales workflows

### Sales Team Enablement
- Supported new sales‑person inductions
- Created onboarding materials and process guidance
- Acted as a point of contact for sales enablement queries

### Sales Target Setting
- Supported annual and quarterly target‑setting exercises
- Aligned targets with strategic and regional priorities

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Analyzed pipeline, win‑loss trends, and account performance
- Generated insights for leadership and sales managers

This work ensured **process consistency and performance transparency**.
