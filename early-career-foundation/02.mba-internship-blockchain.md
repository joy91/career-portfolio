# MBA Internship – Blockchain Strategy & Business Use Cases

## Overview
During my **full‑time MBA program**, I completed a **2‑month internship**
with a leading IT services organization, focused on an **emerging Horizon‑2 technology at the time: Blockchain**.

The objective was not technical implementation, but to **evaluate blockchain’s
business applicability** across industries and create **executive‑ready case studies**
to inform leadership thinking.

---

## Internship Context
- Full‑time MBA program
- Internship duration: 2 months
- Focus area: Blockchain as an emerging enterprise technology
- Nature of work: Strategy, use‑case identification, executive communication

---

## My Role
- Business & technology analyst
- Emerging technology researcher
- Executive presentation contributor

---

## Key Activities

### Technology Understanding
- Studied blockchain fundamentals and enterprise architectures
- Assessed strengths, limitations, and maturity of blockchain platforms
- Evaluated where blockchain adds value vs. traditional systems

### Business Use‑Case Development
Created structured **business case studies** for blockchain adoption across:
- **Finance** (trust, auditability, settlement)
- **Transportation** (tracking, provenance)
- **CPG** (traceability, counterfeit prevention)
- **Supply Chain** (visibility, multi‑party coordination)

Each case focused on:
- Business problem
- Why blockchain is relevant
- Expected business impact
- Risks and adoption considerations

### Executive Communication
- Synthesized findings into **concise, executive‑friendly case studies**
- Presented recommendations to **C‑suite leaders and Chief Business Officers**
- Framed blockchain as a **strategic enabler**, not a technology experiment

---

## Outcomes & Impact
- Leadership appreciated the **clarity and practicality** of the use‑case framing
- Case studies helped decision‑makers understand where blockchain made business sense
- Strengthened my credibility in **technology‑to‑business translation**

---

## How This Shaped My Career
This internship:
- Strengthened my **technology foresight and strategic thinking**
- Reinforced my interest in **data, platforms, and emerging technologies**
- Prepared me to move into **Senior Analyst roles** bridging business and analytics

This experience directly influenced my later work in
AI, data platforms, forecasting, and enterprise strategy.
