# Problem Statement

Most organizations struggle with project execution due to:

- Fragmented tracking across tools (Excel, email, chat)
- Low visibility for leadership
- Inconsistent Agile practices across teams
- Weak linkage between delivery and business outcomes

Existing tools often:
- Over‑optimize for developers
- Under‑serve PMO and operations
- Become rigid at enterprise scale

---

## Core Problem
How do we design a **single system** that supports:
- Agile team execution
- PMO oversight
- Scalable governance
- Actionable metrics

without slowing teams down?
