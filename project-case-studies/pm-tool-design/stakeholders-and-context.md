# Stakeholders & Context

## Primary Users
- Developers / Delivery teams
- Scrum Masters / Project Managers
- Product Owners
- PMO / Program leadership

## Secondary Users
- Operations managers
- Senior leadership
- Audit & compliance teams

---

## Operating Context
- Mid to large enterprise
- Multiple teams and programs
- Mix of Scrum and Kanban
- Reporting required at team, program, and portfolio level

Each stakeholder needs **different views of the same data**.
