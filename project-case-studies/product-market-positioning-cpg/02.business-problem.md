# Business Problem

The client planned to launch a new cosmetics product in a highly
competitive and crowded US market.

Key challenges included:
- Saturated product categories
- Rapidly shifting consumer preferences
- High cost of failed launches
- Unclear differentiation among competing brands

---

## Core Problem
How can the client **position the new product**
with a compelling and differentiated value proposition
that aligns with evolving consumer trends and drives adoption?
