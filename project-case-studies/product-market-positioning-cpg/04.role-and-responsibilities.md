I acted as the **Senior Analyst**, bridging the gap between
client stakeholders and data science teams.

- Gathered and documented business, functional, and technical requirements
- Worked with data scientists to define data needs and tagging logic
- Reviewed and validated data used for model development and evaluation
- Monitored and validated web‑scraping activities for market intelligence
- Conducted walkthroughs of analytical models and insights with stakeholders
- Performed white space and cohort analysis
- Delivered final executive presentation with strategic recommendations
