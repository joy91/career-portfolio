# Product Market Positioning – CPG (Cosmetics) New Product Launch

## Overview
This case study showcases a **data‑driven product market positioning project**
for a **US‑based CPG (cosmetics) client** preparing to launch a new product.

The objective was to identify:
- What benefits consumers truly value
- Which customer segments to target
- Where market white spaces exist
- How the product should be positioned for maximum adoption

Advanced analytics and marketing frameworks were used to support
a high‑impact go‑to‑market strategy.
