# My Role & Responsibilities

I worked as the **Functional Consultant**, owning the project from
requirements to post‑deployment stabilization.

## Key Responsibilities
- Requirement gathering from client stakeholders
- Analysis of existing SAP MM systems
- Creation of Business Requirement Documents (BRD)
- Resource allocation based on availability and priority
- Technical handover and discussions with architects and developers
- Establishment of development and deployment timelines
- Oversight of development efforts
- Unit testing ownership and validation
- Coordination with QA teams for testing completion
- Solution presentations and client approvals
- Coordination of deployment activities
- Post‑deployment defect tracking and analysis
- Change requirement gathering and solution design
- Ticket management in project tracking systems
- Documentation of functional specifications
