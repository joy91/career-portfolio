# Business Problem

The client relied on fragmented SAP MM data for supply chain planning,
making demand and supply forecasting:
- Slow
- Inconsistent
- Heavily manual

Forecasting systems struggled due to:
- Complex SAP MM table structures
- Lack of a unified analytical data layer
- Risk of operational disruption during system changes

---

## Core Problem
How can we create a **centralized DataHub from SAP MM data**
to support accurate demand and supply forecasting,
while ensuring business continuity across factories?
