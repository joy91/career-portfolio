# DataHub Design & Modeling

## SAP MM Data Modeling
- Identified and analyzed key SAP MM tables
- Designed combinations and joins to create a unified data model
- Abstracted complex SAP structures into analytics‑friendly entities

---

## DataHub Objectives
- Provide a single source of truth for supply chain data
- Support downstream demand and supply forecasting models
- Enable scalability across factories

The DataHub acted as the **foundation layer** for forecasting analytics.
