# Modeling Approach

## Why Collaborative Filtering?
Collaborative filtering was chosen because:
- It leverages collective customer behavior
- It adapts as purchase patterns evolve
- It works well for implicit feedback data

---

## Approach Used
- User‑based collaborative filtering
- Similarity computed from purchase behavior
- Recommendations generated based on similar users

RFM metrics were used to:
- Adjust recommendation strength
- Differentiate high‑value vs low‑engagement users

---

## Design Philosophy
- Start simple and interpretable
- Optimize for relevance, not complexity
- Focus on business usability
