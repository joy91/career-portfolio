# Business Problem

E‑commerce platforms often face:
- Low conversion on generic recommendations
- Siloed category‑level suggestions
- Missed cross‑sell and up‑sell opportunities

Customers interact across multiple categories, but
recommendations are frequently:
- Static
- Rule‑based
- Not personalized to buying behavior

---

## Core Problem
How can we design a **personalized recommendation engine**
that:
- Learns from customer purchase behavior
- Surfaces relevant products across categories
- Improves customer lifetime value (CLV)?
