# Business Problem

The client operated a high‑volume railways ticket booking platform with
millions of customer interactions across digital channels.

Key challenges included:
- Fragmented customer data across systems
- Limited personalization in marketing campaigns
- Slow service resolution due to lack of unified customer view
- Increasing regulatory pressure around data privacy (GDPR)

---

## Core Problem
How can we create a **single, unified customer profile**
that enables personalized engagement, operational efficiency,
and regulatory compliance?
