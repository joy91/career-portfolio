# Solution Design & Architecture

## Design Objective
- Unify customer data across channels
- Enable real‑time and batch personalization
- Support marketing and service teams with a 360° customer view

---

## High‑Level Solution
- Salesforce CDP used to ingest, unify, and resolve customer identities
- Creation of unified customer profiles
- Salesforce Marketing Cloud used for campaign activation and personalization
- Consent and preference management embedded to support GDPR compliance
