# My Role & Responsibilities

I worked as the **Functional Analyst** for the project, acting as the
primary bridge between business users, Salesforce vendor teams,
and internal Salesforce cloud implementation teams.

## Key Responsibilities
- Gathered and documented business requirements from client stakeholders
- Translated requirements into functional and technical documentation
- Performed hands‑on configuration in Salesforce CDP using low‑code / no‑code tools
- Coordinated with Salesforce vendor and internal SF cloud teams for implementation
- Conducted solution walkthroughs and demonstrations for the client
- Created user manuals and adoption quizzes for active client users
- Worked closely with the client marketing team on personalization use cases
