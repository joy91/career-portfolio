# Modeling & Analytics Approach

## Sentiment Analysis
- Used **VADER sentiment analysis**
- Classified feedback into positive, neutral, and negative sentiment
- Enabled quick identification of experience pain points and strengths

---

## Topic Modeling
- Applied **Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA)**
- Identified recurring themes across customer feedback
- Mapped themes to product, service, and experience dimensions

---

## Design Philosophy
- Prefer interpretable models
- Focus on business usability over algorithmic complexity
