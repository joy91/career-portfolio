# Business Problem

Organizations manage large volumes of client contracts that contain
critical legal, commercial, and compliance information.

Manual contract review is:
- Time‑consuming
- Error‑prone
- Difficult to scale

Key contract elements are often:
- Inconsistently structured
- Buried in long legal text
- Hard to validate across large contract repositories

---

## Core Problem
How can we use AI to **automatically classify and verify critical contract
components**, reducing manual effort while improving accuracy and governance?
