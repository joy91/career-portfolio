# Learnings & Impact

## Key Learnings
- NLP projects succeed or fail on validation quality
- Business trust matters more than marginal accuracy gains
- Analyst–client communication is critical in AI adoption

## Impact
This project strengthened my ability to:
- Bridge business, legal, and AI teams
- Validate AI outputs with domain context
- Position AI solutions for real‑world adoption

This case reflects my approach to **responsible, business‑aligned AI delivery**.
