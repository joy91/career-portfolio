# Product Recommendation Engine – PoC (Collaborative Filtering + RFM)

## Overview
This case study showcases a **Proof of Concept (PoC)** for a
**product recommendation engine** designed for e‑commerce platforms.

The objective was to:
- Recommend relevant products to consumers
- Enable **cross‑sell and up‑sell** across categories
- Use **purchase history and customer RFM metrics**
- Demonstrate how AI can drive **business outcomes**, not just accuracy

The PoC focuses on **collaborative filtering** as the core modeling approach.
